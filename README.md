# Learning AI  🚀

Welcome to my **Learning AI** repository!

This repository is a public record of my journey into Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI, and AI Engineering. I am learning by building projects, solving problems, experimenting with new technologies, and documenting everything along the way.

The goal is simple: **learn consistently, build publicly, and improve every day.**

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Learn AI and AI Engineering from the ground up.
- Build a strong foundation in Python, mathematics, and data science.
- Understand Machine Learning and Deep Learning concepts.
- Explore Generative AI, LLMs, RAG, and AI Agents.
- Develop real-world AI projects.
- Share progress publicly and track learning over time.
